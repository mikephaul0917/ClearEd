# Clearance System FAQs by Role

This document provides answers to common questions for Students, Officers, Deans, and Administrators within the E-Clearance system.

---

## 🎓 For Students
*Quickly navigate your clearance process and stay on track.*

> [!TIP]
> Always check your "To-Do" list first to see which organizations require your attention.

### Q: What is this platform used for?
**A:** This platform digitizes the university clearance process. It allows you to track your clearance status in real-time, submit required documents digitally, and receive automated notifications when an organization clears you.

### Q: How do I track my clearance progress?
**A:** Navigate to the **"My Clearance"** or **"Progress"** tab in your sidebar. You will see a list of all required organizations and their current status (Pending, Cleared, or with Remarks).

### Q: Why does an organization have a "Remark" on my status?
**A:** If an Officer finds an issue with your submission (e.g., missing document or unpaid fee), they will leave a Remark. Click on the organization to read the specific instructions on how to resolve the issue.

### Q: How do I get my final Clearance Receipt?
**A:** Once all organizations have cleared you and the Dean has provided the final approval, you can download your official **Clearance Receipt** from the "Certificate" tab.

### Q: I forgot my password, what should I do?
**A:** Use the "Forgot Password" link on the login page. If you are already logged in and want to change it, go to **Settings > Password**.

## ✍️ For Officers (Signatories)
*Manage your organization's queue and review student submissions.*

### Q: How do I review a student's clearance?
**A:** Go to your **"To Review"** or **"Dashboard"** page. You will see a list of students pending for your organization. Click on a student to view their details or any uploaded documents.

### Q: What is the difference between "Clearing" and "Leaving Remarks"?
**A:** 
- **Clear:** This marks the student as satisfied for your organization.
- **Remark:** If something is missing, leave a remark. The student will be notified and can take corrective action.

### Q: Can I see a history of students I've cleared?
**A:** Yes, you can toggle your dashboard view to "Reviewed" or "History" to see all students you have processed during the current term.

## 🏛️ For Deans
*Provide the final institutional seal of approval.*

> [!IMPORTANT]
> Students will only appear in your Final Approvals list once **ALL** individual organizations have cleared them.

### Q: How do I perform a final approval?
**A:** Navigate to the **"Final Approvals"** dashboard. Click the "Sign" icon next to a student's name to apply your digital signature and finalize their clearance.

### Q: Can I approve students in bulk?
**A:** Yes. Use the **"Approve All"** or multi-select students and click the bulk approval button. This will apply your signature to all selected records simultaneously.

### Q: How do I update my official digital signature?
**A:** Go to **Settings**. You can either draw your signature directly on the screen or upload a high-quality image. This signature is used for all final clearance receipts.

## ⚙️ For Administrators
*System configuration, user management, and institutional oversight.*

### Q: How do I set up a new Academic Year or Term?
**A:** Navigate to **"AY & Term"** in the sidebar. Here you can create new terms and set the "Active" term. The system uses the active term to determine which clearance requirements apply to students.

### Q: How do I add a new Organization or Department?
**A:** Go to the **"Organizations"** page. You can create new entities, assign them a name, and later assign **Officers** to manage them.

### Q: A user is locked out; how can I help?
**A:** In the **"Users List"** tab, search for the user by name or email. You can manually trigger a password reset link or update their account status if they have been deactivated.

### Q: Where can I monitor system activity?
**A:** Use the **"System Records"** or **"Audit Logs"** page. It maintains a detailed log of all critical actions, including logins, status changes, and administrative overrides.

### Q: How do I handle institution access requests?
**A:** If your system manages multiple campuses, navigate to **"Institution Requests"** to review and approve new campus administrators who wish to join the platform.
